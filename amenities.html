Community Life

More Than Just a Place to Live

What makes Countryside special isn't the buildings ‚Äî it's the people. Our residents organize a full social calendar, especially during the winter season when our snowbird friends are in town.

Typical activities include weekly potluck dinners, country western jam sessions with live music from residents, holiday celebrations, card games and board game nights, shuffleboard and horseshoe tournaments, coffee mornings, and community barbecues.

Participation is always optional, but most residents find themselves drawn in by the friendly atmosphere. It's hard to say no when your neighbor shows up with a casserole and an invitation.
