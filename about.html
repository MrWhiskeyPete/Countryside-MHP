Life in the Rio Grande Valley

The Rio Grande Valley is one of Texas's best-kept secrets for retirees. The cost of living is well below the national average. The weather is warm and sunny nearly year-round. And the culture ‚Äî a blend of Texan and Mexican influences ‚Äî gives the area a character you won't find anywhere else.

Donna sits in Hidalgo County, just minutes from McAllen, Edinburg, and Weslaco. You'll find excellent healthcare facilities, shopping centers, golf courses, birding parks, and easy access to South Padre Island for day trips to the beach. The border crossing to Reynosa, Mexico is just 20 minutes away for affordable dining, shopping, and dental care.

It's no wonder thousands of retirees choose the Valley every year. And once they find Countryside, they keep coming back.
